Sponsor us

Partner with the Cam's most successful club

The Boat Club is looking for corporate sponsors for both club equipment and activities, and for the annual Fairbairn Cup — the largest rowing event organised by a Cambridge college club.

Rowing is the most popular sport in Cambridge, with over a third of the University involved and many more turning out to watch. Jesus is one of the most accomplished clubs on the river, with success both on the Cam and further afield.

Sponsoring the Club is an excellent opportunity for recruitment and for getting your name recognised by thousands of students at the University.

Why sponsor JCBC?

  • › A third of the University rows — huge, engaged reach.
  • › Naming exposure at the Fairbairn Cup (≈250 crews).
  • › Direct recruitment access to Cambridge students.
  • › Association with one of the river's top clubs.
