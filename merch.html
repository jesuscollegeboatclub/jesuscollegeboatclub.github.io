How it works

Add what you'd like to your basket and check out online — kit is produced in seasonal batches, so delivery times are shown at checkout. Current members can also collect from the boathouse to save on postage.

All proceeds go straight back into the club: new boats, blades and supporting our rowers.

Good to know

Sizing: unisex unless stated — a size guide appears on each product.

Bicentenary range: strictly limited; some items are pre-order for 2027 delivery.
