Constitution

The Club's constitution

The Constitution of Jesus College Boat Club was ratified on 19/10/2019 and amended on 02/06/2020, 08/05/2021, 29/04/2022, 21/10/2023 and 13/01/2025. It sets out the Club's objectives, its officers and committee, membership, elections, meetings, finances and disciplinary procedures.
