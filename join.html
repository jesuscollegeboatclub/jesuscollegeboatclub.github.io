For beginners

Rowing at Jesus

The boat club is, by a considerable margin, the largest and most popular sporting society at the college — and possibly the largest in the whole university. Over 90% of people who join have never rowed before, so we have one of the best setups in the country for coaching beginners. It's also the only chance most people get to take up the sport completely for free.

  • Genuinely open to anyone — no subscription fees, everyone welcomed straight in.
  • Space for every commitment level, from a casual paddle to training for the Boat Race.
  • Well funded, with an unparalleled range of boats.
  • A full-time head coach and boatman, plus an onsite physio.
  • Boathouse just minutes' cycle from college — closer than any other.
  • One of the liveliest social scenes in college, including termly boat club dinners.
Bumps — the highlight of the year. A chaotic style of racing unique to Oxbridge: boats line up and chase the crew in front, trying to 'bump' them before being caught from behind. Over four days, crews rise and fall through the order, chasing the coveted blades.
For experienced rowers

Rowing for the University

Cambridge University Boat Club is one of the most successful in the world, competing against Oxford each year in the world-famous Boat Race; its rowers regularly go on to U23 and Olympic level. Jesus is the most successful college on the river, and almost every year has multiple rowers in the university squads — the previous two Women's Presidents were both Jesus members.

  • Financial support for university-squad rowers — extra tenancy, kit, travel and more.
  • A full-time dedicated college head coach to reach the required technical standard.
  • Unparalleled fleet, from singles to new eights — largely Filippi and Empacher.
  • Free-to-use gym and ergo room: extensive weights, 16 ergs and 3 spin bikes.
  • Onsite college physio — unique among Cambridge colleges.
  • Heavily subsidised annual winter training camp in Mequinenza, Spain.
  • No membership or subscription fees, and heavily discounted kit.
  • Guaranteed accommodation minutes from both boathouses, for every year of study.
